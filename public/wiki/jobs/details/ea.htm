We are a global team of creators, storytellers, technologists, experience originators, innovators and so much more. We believe amazing games and experiences start with teams as diverse as the players and communities we serve. At Electronic Arts, the only limit is your imagination.

The EA Digital Platform (EADP) group is the core powering the global EA ecosystem. We provide the foundation for all of EA’s incredible games and player experiences with high-level platforms like Cloud, Commerce, Data and AI, Gameplay Services, Identity and Social. By providing reusable capabilities that game teams can easily integrate into their work, we let them focus on making some of the best games in the world and creating meaningful relationships with our players. We’re behind the curtain, making it all work together. Come power the future of play with us.

The Challenge Ahead includes building our new cross-game, cross-platform digital economy platform. This large-scale, always-on collection of Java/Kotlin and GO-based services will power the virtual-economies of EA’s network of games, experienced by tens of millions of monthly active users. You will build platform capabilities handling several hundred-thousand requests per second, driving the player engagement of many of the industry's most popular titles. You will build server-side systems that deliver new functionality within the larger Digital Platform and work on regular improvements to the systems to keep them at top-notch performance.

As a Senior Software Engineer, you will report to the Director of Engineering. You will lead the software design and architecture process of our services, while optimizing performance and scalability. You will work with product managers to develop new features, supporting EA's many games and reducing technical debt associated with legacy systems through innovation and automation.

Responsibilities:

  • You will lead the technical design and architecture of major components in our large-scale distributed backend service, creating new features and updating existing ones.
  • You will work with customers and business leaders to understand customer needs and delivering incrementally on their highest priority features and requirements.
  • You will spend 40%-60% of your time hands-on coding.
  • You will provide occasional off-hours support for development needs and the live environment.

The Technology

Our technology teams comprise of two core groups:

  • Enterprise & Platform Services (EPS). EPS defines and delivers technology that drives EA's enterprise and live services,
  • Creative & Development (C&D) which partners with studios to build creative tools and solutions for our games.

Within these two groups we have the following tech teams:

  • Data and Aanalytics (EPS): maximizing user experience (players and game makers) through AI drive solutions. Also automate tasks related to testing, data management and development prioritization.
  • Game Engine Technology and Tools (C&D): includes Frostbite engine, allowing EA game makers to do their best creative work
  • Infrastructure and Platform services (EPS): ensure developers have infrastructure, support and reusable capabilities so they can focus on the creative experiences. As gaming moves to the cloud this team delivers components for gameplay services, commerce, social systems, data capture and management
  • Research, Innovation and Growth: a cross discipline team of elite ressearchers and talent empowering EA to adapt to the evolving tech landscape, develop external partnerships, evolve creative development and future interactive/live entertainment.
  • Security and IT: to protect employees, plaers and online spaces - so both at player and entrprise levels. Proves education and conducts threat assessments so that all teams at EA have safe and reliable access to the tools to do their work.
  • Techmology Operations: the hub across all teams to accelerate technology development to empower greatest quality experiences for players. They deliver insights, change management, build tools, processes and capabilities to support the overall Tech organization.
