Opt Health - Senior Software Engineer at San Fran - Remote

Opt Health is looking for an experienced and passionate senior software engineer to join our team. You will be responsible for the development and maintenance of our platform infrastructure, frontend services and APIs that power our product and services at Opt Health. In this role you will lead the design, implementation, and deployment of key initiatives and guide fellow team members.

The ideal candidate will be able to quickly turn ideas from inception to final product in a fast-paced and friendly environment. You should be comfortable learning new technologies as needed.

Responsibilities

  • Take ownership of the NodeJS platform's infrastructure and lead efforts to enhance its security, scalability, and reliability.
  • Develop an architectural understanding of the application as a whole (FE, BE, and integrations) and contribute in efforts to improving overall application architecture.
  • Mentor and guide junior engineers, fostering a culture of positive, continuous learning and improvement.

Qualifications

  • At least 7 years experience as a Software Engineer working on web applications
  • Proven experience as a Senior Software Engineer
  • Experience with JavaScript and NodeJS, along with experience in building and maintaining scalable backend systems.
  • Experience with SQL and Postgres.
  • Some experience or familiarity with React or similar front-end systems preferred.
  • Strong proficiency breaking down complex engineering tasks into manageable sub-tasks, demonstrating a systematic and organized approach to problem-solving.
  • Solid understanding of software architecture and design patterns for web applications.
  • Effective communication skills and the ability to work collaboratively in a fast-paced startup environment.
