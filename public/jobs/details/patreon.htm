Patreon - Senior Software Engineer, Payments

About the job

Patreon is the best place for creators to build exclusive content and community for their fans. We enable creators (podcasters, writers, musicians, illustrators, etc) to connect with their fans directly and make money from their creative work. Creators can sell one-off items from their own shops or offer recurring monthly memberships with exclusive access to their work.

There are over 250,000 active creators earning on Patreon and over 10 million members. Our goal has always been to help creators achieve financial freedom doing what they love - and we're leaders in the space, with over $3.5 billion earned by creators on our platform. We’re continuing to invest heavily in building the most talented team in the Creator Economy and are looking for a Software Engineer to support our mission.

This role is based in San Francisco and open to those who are able to be in-office 2 days per week on a hybrid work model.

About The Role

  • You'll help scale and expand capabilities of Patreon's payment platform that helps creators get paid effectively and on time, every time.
  • Lead and contribute to the development and design of the Payments platform and tools to support the payment processing at Patreon.
  • Collaborate with platform and infrastructure teams to improve our automated testing and reliability of our payments platform.
  • Collaborate with product managers, designers, and other engineers to build new functionality, while maintaining a delightful user experience, motivated by passion for our shared mission.
  • Work with a supportive team to get things done with the right mix of quality and agility.
  • Work with Java, Python, Flask, Spring Boot, MySQL, and AWS, introducing other technologies when it makes sense.
  • Build a strong product-minded, engineering culture by mentoring and guiding engineers inside and outside the team

About You

  • 5+ years of experience in backend software development
  • Excited to maintain high standard of quality and craft with an eye for user experience and engineering detail
  • A track record of building systems with attention to detail, high reliability, and high throughput
  • Experience designing, building, deploying, monitoring and maintaining code in a service-oriented architecture
  • Proactive in communicating with your peers and cross-functional partners
Experience building products in the payments domain is a plus, but not a strict requirement for this role
