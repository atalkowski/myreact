Akkodis - Senior Software Engineer

About the job

Akkodis is looking for a Sr. Software Engineer to join the team of our client in one of the following locations; San Francisco, Los Angeles, or NYC. This individual will be responsible for modeling the commerce data for streaming products and running transformations adapting it to a variety of formats for consumers. Experience with Backend Development, Functional Programming, and Data Conversion are required.

Pay Range: $70/hr-$86/hr

Responsibilities

  • Create new data models and extend existing ones to support new business initiatives representing packaging, pricing, and promotional aspects of various digital products.
  • Design solutions that interconnect existing commerce systems from various organizations, allowing for bundling of products from disparate business units.
  • Create scalable backend services in Scala/JVM that support traceability of incremental data mutations and fast recovery from any undesired data state.
  • Be involved in code reviews and architecture reviews for team members.
  • Participate in Agile methodology and SCRUM processes such as Sprint Planning,
  • Backlog grooming, Daily Standups, and Retrospectives with the team

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of experience in backend services development
  • Proficient in designing and implementing complex data architectures and systems.
  • Experience working with cloud technologies (AWS preferred).
  • Knowledge of Scala/JVM-based languages. Functional programming experience is a strong plus.
  • In-depth experience working on relational and graph databases, mapping complex logical models into persistence layers (PostgreSQL, MySQL).
  • Familiarity with Window/Partition functions is a plus.
  • Previous experience taking ownership of features/functionality and delivering projects end-to-end with high-quality and robust architecture.
  • Excellent communication skills and ability to work with cross-functional business partners.
  • Ability to dive deep into any technical component as well as understand and drive the overall systems architecture.
